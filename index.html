Recipe #1:Sponge Cake

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 8 people
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 4
Sugar 1/2 cup
Vanilla Extract 1/2 teaspoon
Lemon Zest 1 teaspoon
Flour 1/2 cup
Salt 1/4 teaspoon
Tartar Cream 1/4 teaspoon

Step 1: Preheat oven to 325

Step 2: Grease only the bottom of a 9-inch round cake pan and cover with a circle of parchment paper. Do not grease the sides of the pan.

Step 3: In a large bowl, beat egg yolks until light, gradually adding in the sugar. Add in vanilla and lemon and set aside.

Step 4: In a medium bowl, beat the egg whites until frothy, about 1 minute at high speed, then beat in the salt and cream of tartar. Continue beating until the eggs reach soft peaks. Fold egg whites, working in 3 or 4 batches, into the yolk mixture.

Step 5: Sift the flour over the egg mixture, again working in 3 or 4 batches, and gently fold it inches When the batter is uniform, pour into prepared cake pan and bake unil a tester comes out dry and the top of the cake springs back when gently pressed, about 35-45 minutes.

Step 6: Allow cake to cool in the pan before cutting around the sides with a sharp knife and turning it out.

Step 7: Slice the cake into 2 or 3 layers (or squares) and fill as desired.
